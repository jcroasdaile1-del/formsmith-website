❓ Help & FAQ

How does the handicap system work? ▼
Each week, the app compares each team's running average (from their previous 2 weeks of scores) to their opponent's average. The weaker team gets a handicap: (Opponent Avg − Team Avg) × 80%. The stronger team gets 0 handicap. For example, an 800-avg team vs a 700-avg team → the 700 team gets an 80-point handicap (100 × 0.8). The adjusted scores (raw + handicap) determine W/L/T.
How are brackets determined? ▼
During qualifying weeks (first 3 by default), all teams shoot. After qualifying, click "🔒 Lock Brackets" on the Teams tab. Teams are ranked by their qualifying average and split evenly into brackets (A = strongest, B = next, etc.).
How does the schedule work? ▼
After locking brackets, a round-robin schedule is auto-generated. With 10 teams per bracket, each team plays every other team once over a 9-week cycle. If bracket play runs longer than one cycle, the round-robin pairings repeat. In a bracket with an odd number of teams, one team each week shoots against its own running average.
What counts as a tie? ▼
If two teams' adjusted scores (raw + handicap) are exactly equal, it's a tie. Each team gets 0.5 wins added to their record.
Can I print weekly standings? ▼
Yes! On the Scores tab, select a week and click "🖨️ Print Week". This generates a print-friendly view with individual scores, team totals, handicaps, results, and division standings.
How do I start a new season? ▼
Back up your current data first (💾 Backup button), then clear localStorage by going to your browser's developer tools or simply clearing site data. Each season is a fresh start.
